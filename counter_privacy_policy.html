📌 1. Introduction

Welcome to Counter App — a minimal, elegant tool designed to help you count anything, anytime. This privacy policy explains how we treat your data. We believe in absolute transparency: no tracking, no servers, no analytics. Your counter data belongs only to you.

📀 2. Data Collection & Storage

Counter App collects zero personal data. All your counter values and preferences are stored locally on your device using browser's localStorage. We never transmit, share, or sell any information.

✨ What we DON'T collect:
  • ❌ No email, name, or IP address
  • ❌ No analytics or cookies (except functional localStorage)
  • ❌ No third-party tracking scripts
  • ❌ No crash reports or device info

🧠 3. How The Counter Works

The counter value you see (and interact with) is saved using your browser's built-in storage. If you clear your browser data, the counter resets. No external database, no cloud sync — just pure local persistence. You are always in control.

  • ✅ Increment / decrement actions are processed locally.
  • ✅ The "Reset" button clears the stored value inside your browser.
  • ✅ You can use the app offline, 100% functional.

🔐 4. Security & Your Rights

Because your data never leaves your device, we don't store or process it externally. You have the right to inspect, modify, or delete your counter data anytime by clearing your browser's localStorage or using the "Reset" button. For any privacy-related curiosity, contact our support (email below).

🧸 5. Children's Privacy

Counter App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app is simple, safe, and contains no harmful content, advertisements, or data collection features. Parents can rest assured.

🔄 6. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this policy (for example to improve clarity), we will update the "Effective date" above. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and continued use of the app implies agreement. But note — our core principle remains: never collecting data.
